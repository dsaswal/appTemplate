About Role Profiles

What are Role Profiles?

Role profiles allow you to bundle multiple roles into a single profile. This makes it easier to assign multiple roles to users at once.

Examples:

  • Sales Manager: MANAGER + SALES + CRM_ACCESS
  • Regional Director: DIRECTOR + MULTI_REGION_ACCESS + BUDGET_APPROVAL
  • Developer Team Lead: DEVELOPER + TEAM_LEAD + DEPLOYMENT_ACCESS

Users can be assigned both individual roles AND profiles for maximum flexibility.
